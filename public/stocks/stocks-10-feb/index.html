Nifty & Bank Nifty Levels:

Why did Nifty fall in the end?#


This was a operator driven movement to fight something which is called a "Theta Decay". When market stays for some time on the same level, the premiums of options reduce, even tho Nifty is the same.
This is due to "Theta Decay".

Nifty Levels:#

Nifty is completely bullish till it satys above 15100. 15100 is a strong support for Nifty. After that, 15000 is extremely strong.

  1. Nifty Opens Above 15150: Green Candle: Enjoy Rally. Red Candle: 15100 is support
  2. Nifty Opens around 15100: Will slowly move up for the day
  3. Nifty Opens below 15100: 15000 will be a strong unbreakable support

Bank Nifty Levels:#

36000 is a support and 36500 & 37000 are targets for Bank Nifty. But it is too volatile. There is no good trade possible for Bank Nifty.
