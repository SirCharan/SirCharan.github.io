15th-19th Feb | Weekly Market View

Feb 14 2021

Nifty & Bank Nifty Levels:

Nifty & Bank Nifty both are range bound, but a big move is coming soon!

Nifty Levels For The Week:#

Nifty is bullish, and ready for a breakout.

  1. 15,100 & below till 15,000: Nifty is strong above 15,100, and 15100 is a strong support. It will be bullish till it stays above that level. If 15,100 is broken, the next level coming will be 15,000, which is a extremely strong unbreakable support.

  2. 15,250 and above to 15,500: On the upside, 15,250 is a strong resistance which Nifty is not able to break right now. If that is broken, we will see a big rally upside and Nifty will touch 15,400 and 15,500 levels in 2-3 days only after that.

  3. Between 15,100 &15,250: Till then, Nifty gonna stay moving up and down between 15,100 and 15,250, like we are seeing it consolidating in this range.

How to Trade In Nifty:#

How to Trade between 15,100 to 15,250: Simple strategy, buy on 15,100 and target 15,250. You can buy any CE you want in range of 15,100-15,200.

How to Trade for above 15,250: Buy for breakout. You can buy any CE upto 15,400, and if you have a 15,100 to 15,200 ki CE, you can hold it too.

Minor Trades: If you see Nifty is breaking at the end of day, you can buy a PE. But hold only for a short time, reversals will be fast.

Bank Nifty Levels For The Week:#

Bank Nifty is not as strong as Nifty, but it will gain strength in later part of the week.

  1. Below 36,000: 36,000 is a strong pivot level for Bank Nifty. It is bearish below this level and bullish above this level. 35,800 is first support. Then 35,600 is second support.

  2. Above 36,000: When Bank Nifty is above 36,000, do not take bearish(short) trades in any bank. 36,500 is a resistance for Bank Nifty

  3. Above 36,500: Bank Nifty is facing a major resistance at 36,500. We will see a big rally when that level is crossed. All banks look ready for a upward move soon too…

How to Trade In Bank Nifty:#

How to trade for below 36,000: Buy any PE, 35,600 and 35,800 are support levels, look to exit there.

How to trade for above 36,000: Buy any CE, 36,500 is a resistance. If broken, enjoy!
