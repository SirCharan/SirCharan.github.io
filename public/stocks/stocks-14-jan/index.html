Nifty Levels#

Nifty looks to touch 14500 in this week. [Already Touched! Next week target is 14800!]
Nifty may experience profit booking from 14600 levels, every dip is a buying oppoutunity [We saw that today!]

Market is still completely bullish.

  2. Nifty touching 14600 from below: 14600 is a strong resistance with 4.2 million contracts. You can book profits at this level.
  3. Nifty touching 14600 from above: 14600 is not a strong, so may break easily.
  4. Nifty touching 14500 from above: 14500 will now act as strong support, so if nifty comes down and takes support, buy a CE. 14600 is strong resistance.
  5. Risk Takers: Short if first candle is bearish, and short if Nifty faces resistance oon 14600. Suport on 14500. Also, play by channel.(Risky)
