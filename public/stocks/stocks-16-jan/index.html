Nifty & Bank Nifty Levels:

Nifty Levels#

Nifty looks to have exhausted its bullish run temporarily, so the channel of 14400-14500 will be important. Buy on dips will still work, but market is not fully bullish now.

  1. First Candle: Watch candle. If bullish, buy CE. Targets will update live in market. 14500 is strong resistance. If bearish, let the support on 14400 break first.
  2. Nifty breaks below 14400: 14400 is a strong support, if it breaks, take a bearish trade. 14350, 14300 will be your targets.
  3. Nifty touches 14500 from below: 14500 is a strong resistance. You can look to book profits at this level. If it breaks above 14500, buy fresh.
